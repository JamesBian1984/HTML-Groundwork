General/Universal Mechanics

For new players that are only beginning to learn the unique mechanics that guilty gear offers. Unlike other conventional fighting games, there are a variety of mechanics that players must take advantage of that are shared amongst most or all characters alike. Knowing the general rules and interactions of all mechanics allows for a stronger and more knowledgeable player.
